The writer: handsome, clever, and rich, with a comfortable home and perplexed disposition, seems to unite some of the best and worst blessings of existence; and has lived nearly one hundred twenty-one years in the world with very little to distress or vex him.

Experience

  • Fiction: Written three novels and 12 short stories in the science fiction genre, a graphic superhero novel, and a soon-to-be-released fourth science fiction novel.
  • Non-Fiction: Written three books covering topics in philosophy, natural healing, and homeopathy.

Awards

  • Winner of the 2022 Nebulous Award for his novel, Butterfly Women of Arboris Six.
  • Winner, 2021 Philip K. Dick Award for most original science fiction paperback for his novel, Attack of the Arachnoids.
